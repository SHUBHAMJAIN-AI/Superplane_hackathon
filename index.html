About

I’m Shubham Jain, currently CTO at ShubLabs.ai, where I focus on dependable AI infrastructure for developer tools. My work spans multi-tenant inference gateways, prompt evaluation frameworks, safety guardrails, and real-time observability. I enjoy turning ambiguous product goals into crisp architectures, and I care deeply about latency, cost efficiency, and great developer experience.

I’ve led small, high-ownership teams; hired and mentored engineers; and shipped distributed systems in TypeScript, Python, and Go running on Kubernetes. I’m energized by systems that blend LLM Ops with strong platform engineering.
